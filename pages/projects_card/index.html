Projects

Water & Wastewater Rehabilitation

City of Weatherford

J&L Construction was responsible for performing a water and wastewater rehabilitation project along different city roads. This involved rehabilitating 6,500 linear feet of waterline and 4,600 linear feet of sewer line, including 4,500 square yards of asphalt and concrete pavement reconstruction. The project was completed in 6 months.

Bait Shop and South Orchard Rd Channel

Lucas, TX

The City of Lucas awarded J&L two projects. The Bait Shop project consisted of 4000 linear feet of 12” waterline relocation along city streets, including 300 steel casing bores. The South Orchard Rd channel was a storm drainage system improvement, which consisted of 1800 LF of 4’ width concrete channel and 34 linear feet of box culverts with Rip Rap devices.

Water Line Replacement

Trophy Club MUD No 1

The Trophy Club MUD awarded J&L Construction the task of replacing water lines along different city roads. In this project, we replaced 5000 linear feet of 8” water lines, including open cut and directional bore methods. It was completed in six months.

Construction of Waterline Improvements Project

South Grayson Special Utility District

This project consisted of constructing a new water line along Van Alstyne streets. J&L Construction installed 10,500 linear feet of 4” to 8” water lines, including fittings, valves, and all related devices.
